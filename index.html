Development

Process

This section explains how the work develops from rough ideas into detailed drawings. Universities often value seeing how you think, not only the final image.

How I Develop a Piece

1

Concept

I begin with a symbol, object, creature, or strange image that could suggest a story.

2

Composition

I test scale, balance, and placement so the drawing feels dramatic and readable.

3

Texture

I build tone using repeated lines, hatching, cross-hatching, and dense mark-making.

4

Final Details

I add small symbols, shadows, borders, and focal points to make the piece feel complete.
